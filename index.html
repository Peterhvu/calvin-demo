ABOUT

Tempo Homes is a Minnesota Residential Construction Company specializing in Unique Custom Modern designed homes and residential spaces. We provide service to the entire Twin Cities metro area and surrounding Wisconsin border. Our team is made up of experienced Licensed Contractors, architects/designers, and engineers.

TEMPO LIFESTYLE

Our main goal at Tempo Homes is to introduce a new design concept to the typical real estate market and making

it a lifestyle. Real quality, unique character and a simplistic design with a modern twist are just a few of the passions we consider when developing each and every new custom build. Every home is carefully tailored and constructed specific to the site intended unlike many homes built in your every day neighborhood. For more information, please feel free to inquire within.
